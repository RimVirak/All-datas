Send Contact Form 7, Contact Forms Entries data and many other contact form submissions to Constant Contact.

== Description ==

Contact Form 7 Constant Contact Plugin sends form submissions from [Contact Form 7](https://wordpress.org/plugins/contact-form-7/), [Contact Forms Entries Plugin](https://wordpress.org/plugins/contact-form-entries/) and many other popular contact form plugins to Constant Contact. Learn more at [crmperks.com](https://www.crmperks.com/plugins/contact-form-plugins/contact-form-constant-contact-plugin/?utm_source=wordpress&utm_medium=directory&utm_campaign=constant-contact_readme)


== How to Setup ==

* Go to "Constant Contact Accounts" tab and add new account.
* Go to "Constant Contact Feeds" tab , create new feed.
* Map required Constant Contact fields to Contact Form 7 fields.
* Send your test entry to Constant Contact.
* Go to "Constant Contact Logs" tab and verify, if entry was sent to Constant Contact.


**Connect Constant Contact account**

Connect any contact form 7 to Constant Contact account by simply entering Constant Contact access token. You can connect multiple constant contact accounts.

**Map Constant Contact fields**

Easily map any contact form 7 fields to Constant Contact fields.

**Filter contact form 7  submissions**

Filter contact form 7 submissions sent to Constant Contact based on user input. For example , send those customers to Constant Contact who check "Subscribe" checkbox. 

**Manually send to Constant Contact**

Send contact form 7 submissions to Constant Contact on form submission. You can manually send contact form 7 submissions to Constant Contact.

**Constant Contact logs**

View a detailed log of each contact form 7 submission sent (or not sent) to Constant Contact and easily resend contact form 7 submission to Constant Contact.

**Send Data As Constant Contact object Notes**

Send one to many contact form 7 fields as Constant Contact object notes.
